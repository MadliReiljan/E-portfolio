About Me

I am currently studying at Tartu Vocational Centre, training to become a junior software developer. My interest in coding began with a love for gaming and technology, and over time it grew into a real passion for development. With a background in art, I enjoy bringing creativity into tech—blending design with functionality to create applications that are both visually appealing and effective.

Beyond coding, I am a big nature lover and find myself most at peace outdoors, especially by the ocean. There is something about the calm waves and fresh air that inspires and recharges me. I also have a passion for plants and enjoy caring for my growing collection—they are my little piece of nature indoors.

I am a friendly, easygoing person who loves working with others. Whether it is bouncing ideas around, solving problems or just creating something cool together, I am all for team projects and enjoy connecting with people. I think the best ideas come when we all chip in together.
